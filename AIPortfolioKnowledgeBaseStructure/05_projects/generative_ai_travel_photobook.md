# Generative AI Travel Photobook

## Project Overview
The Generative AI Travel Photobook project explored how artificial intelligence could assist users in transforming large collections of travel photos into structured, story-driven photobooks. The system analyzes image content, identifies meaningful moments, and automatically generates layouts that users can refine and customize.

The goal was to reduce the friction of organizing hundreds of photos while preserving the creative control and storytelling that makes travel memories meaningful.

## Project Scope
Design an AI-powered system that analyzes travel images and automatically generates a complete photobook including image grouping, story structure, and layout suggestions.

## Problem
Travelers often accumulate hundreds or thousands of photos from a trip, but organizing those images into a meaningful photobook can be time-consuming and overwhelming.

Users face several challenges:

- sorting large photo collections  
- identifying key moments from a trip  
- organizing images into a cohesive narrative  
- designing layouts without design expertise  

These challenges often prevent users from completing photobooks, leaving memories unorganized and unused.

## Users
- Travelers documenting personal trips  
- Photography enthusiasts  
- Families organizing travel memories  

## Design Strategy
Design an AI-assisted workflow that transforms raw photo collections into structured photobook drafts while allowing users to remain in control of the final design.

The system focuses on **human–AI collaboration**, where AI accelerates the organization and layout process while users refine and personalize the final output.

## Key Features

### Photo Analysis
The system analyzes uploaded images using computer vision to identify:

- locations and landmarks  
- people and faces  
- key travel moments  
- environmental context (sunset, beach, city, etc.)

This allows the system to automatically group related images and detect significant moments from the trip.

### Story Generation
Based on the analyzed images, the system generates a narrative structure that organizes photos into logical travel chapters such as:

- arrival and early exploration  
- key locations or excursions  
- memorable moments  
- closing reflections  

The goal is to transform scattered photos into a coherent travel story.

### Layout Automation
The AI generates multiple layout options for each section of the photobook, automatically arranging photos into visually balanced page compositions.

Users can then:

- adjust image placements  
- swap photos  
- modify layout styles  
- remove or add pages  

This approach reduces the effort of starting from a blank page while maintaining creative flexibility.

## Human-AI Interaction Design
A central design challenge was ensuring that automation supported creativity rather than replacing it.

The system was designed so that:

- AI provides suggestions rather than final decisions  
- users can easily modify generated layouts  
- the system explains why images were grouped together  
- users retain control over the final design  

This approach helps maintain trust and prevents the system from feeling overly automated.

## Systems Thinking
The product required designing interactions across several interconnected system components:

- image analysis and tagging  
- photo grouping algorithms  
- narrative generation logic  
- layout generation engine  
- user editing workflows  

Rather than focusing only on interface design, the project explored how intelligent systems behave and how users interact with automated outputs.
