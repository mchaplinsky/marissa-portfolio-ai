# NCCS Enterprise Service Platform

## Project Overview
The NCCS project focused on redesigning an enterprise service ordering platform used by government employees to request internal services and resources. The system supports employees across multiple departments who rely on the platform to locate services, submit requests, and track fulfillment through internal operational teams.

The redesign aimed to simplify the service ordering process, improve navigation across a large catalog of internal services, and reduce confusion that often resulted in incorrect service requests or support tickets.

## Project Scope
Redesign the enterprise service ordering experience, improving how employees discover services, submit requests, and track service fulfillment through internal operational workflows.

## Problem
The existing ordering platform was difficult to navigate and relied on complex forms and poorly organized service listings. Users often struggled to find the correct services and frequently submitted incorrect requests.

Key challenges included:

- unclear service categorization  
- confusing navigation structure  
- inefficient ordering workflows  
- high reliance on internal support teams to resolve incorrect requests  

These issues created delays in service fulfillment and increased operational workload for support teams.

## Design Strategy
Redesign the platform to create a clearer and more intuitive service discovery and ordering experience.

The design focused on improving how users search for services, understand available options, and submit requests through simplified workflows.

## Key UX Decisions
- Predictive search to help users quickly locate services  
- Simplified service ordering workflows that guide users step by step  
- Clear service categorization and information hierarchy  
- Service management dashboard to track request status  

These decisions helped reduce confusion and improved how users navigate the service catalog.

## Systems Thinking
The NCCS platform sits within a broader operational ecosystem that includes service teams, fulfillment processes, and internal government systems.

Designing the platform required understanding how service requests move through internal workflows and ensuring the interface supported both request submission and operational management.
