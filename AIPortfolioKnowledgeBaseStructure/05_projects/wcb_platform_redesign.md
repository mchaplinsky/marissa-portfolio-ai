## Project Scope\
The project involved conducting a full UX audit of the WCB website, including accessibility testing, analytics review, and extensive user research. The insights informed a complete redesign of the information architecture.\
\
## Problem\
Users struggled to find critical information on the website, leading to a high volume of calls to the call center and operational inefficiencies.\
