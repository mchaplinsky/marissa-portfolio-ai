\f0\fs24 \cf0 # CMHA IRAS Platform\
\
## Project Overview\
The IRAS platform was designed to provide accessible online resources related to involuntary mental health treatment while also creating internal portals that support communication between staff, facilities, and rights advisors.\
\
## Industry\
Mental Health / Healthcare\
\
## Organization\
Canadian Mental Health Association\
\
## My Role\
Lead UX Designer\
\
## Project Scope\
Design a new public website and internal portals supporting the IRAS program.\
\
## Problem\
The IRAS department needed a platform that provided clear public information while supporting internal communication workflows.\
\
## Users\
Patients and families  \
Rights advisors  \
CMHA staff  \
Healthcare facilities\
\
## Research Methods\
Stakeholder workshops  \
Design iterations  \
Internal feedback sessions\
\
## Design Strategy\
Create a simple and accessible public platform supported by internal administrative portals.\
\
## Key UX Decisions\
Dedicated IRAS branding system  \
Simplified navigation for sensitive content  \
Role-based internal portals\
\
## Systems Thinking\
The platform had to support both public users and internal case management workflows.\
\
## Deliverables\
Design system  \
Prototypes  \
Internal portal wireframes\
\
## Tools Used\
Figma  \
Component libraries\
\
## Impact\
Improved access to mental health rights information  \
Created new communication workflows for staff}
