\f0\fs24 \cf0 # SPD Criminal Record Check System\
\
## Project Overview\
The SPD Criminal Record Check Program redesign focused on modernizing the application and case management experience for the Securities Program Division in British Columbia. The goal was to replace fragmented processes with a streamlined digital system that improved transparency for applicants while reducing operational workload for internal staff.\
\
## Industry\
Government / Public Safety\
\
## Organization\
Securities Program Division \'96 Government of British Columbia\
\
## My Role\
Senior UX Designer\
\
## Project Scope\
The project involved redesigning the entire criminal record check application system and creating a new portal for both applicants and internal staff. The redesign also consolidated more than 40 licensing types into simplified application workflows.\
\
## Problem\
Applicants frequently contacted the organization to check the status of their applications, creating operational strain for staff. The existing system relied heavily on manual processes and lacked transparency for users.\
\

## Design Strategy\
The redesign focused on simplifying the application process while providing users with clear visibility into their application status.\
\
## Key UX Decisions\
Consolidated 40+ license types into two streamlined application flows  \
Introduced role-based portals for applicants and staff  \
Implemented dynamic forms based on user responses  \
Added progress tracking and application transparency\
\
## Systems Thinking\
The platform required coordination across multiple licensing systems and user roles. Service blueprints were used to map the end-to-end workflow and ensure alignment between applicant experiences and internal case management processes.\
