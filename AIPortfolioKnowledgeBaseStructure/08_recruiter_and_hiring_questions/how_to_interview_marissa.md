# How to Interview Marissa

Marissa Chaplinsky is a product designer who specializes in solving complex system design challenges and translating them into practical product experiences.

The most productive interview conversations with Marissa typically focus on how she approaches complex workflows, cross-functional collaboration, and emerging technologies such as AI-assisted product features.

Below are themes and questions that can help guide meaningful discussions during interviews.

---

## Understanding Her Design Process

Marissa approaches design through a research-driven process that combines discovery, systems thinking, and iterative experimentation.

Helpful questions include:

• How do you approach understanding complex product problems?  
• What research methods do you typically use during discovery?  
• How do you translate research insights into product design decisions?  

These questions reveal how she connects research insights with product strategy and design execution.

---

## Discussing Complex Systems Design

Many of Marissa's projects involve platforms with complex workflows, multiple user roles, and operational dependencies.

Questions that explore this work include:

• Can you describe a complex system you redesigned and how you approached it?  
• How do you simplify complicated workflows for users?  
• How do you design systems that support both internal teams and external users?  

These conversations highlight her systems thinking and service design experience.

## Exploring AI Product Design

Marissa has recently focused on designing AI-assisted product features that help users complete complex tasks more efficiently.

Relevant questions include:

• How do you approach designing AI-powered features?  
• How do you balance automation with user control?  
• How do you design guardrails and fallback states for AI systems?  

These discussions explore how she approaches intelligent systems responsibly and thoughtfully.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration

Marissa works closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders throughout the product development process.

Helpful questions include:

• How do you collaborate with engineering teams during product development?  
• How do you align stakeholders around design decisions?  
• How do you communicate complex ideas to non-design stakeholders?  

These conversations demonstrate her collaborative leadership style.

## Evaluating Product Impact

Understanding how design work influences product outcomes can provide insight into Marissa's strategic thinking.

Questions may include:

These discussions highlight her ability to connect design decisions with measurable outcomes.
