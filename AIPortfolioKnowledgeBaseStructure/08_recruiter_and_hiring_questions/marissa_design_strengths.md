# Marissa's Design Strengths

Marissa Chaplinsky’s design strengths center on her ability to understand complex systems and translate them into clear, scalable product experiences.

Her work often focuses on platforms that involve multiple workflows, user roles, and operational processes. By analyzing how these elements interact, she creates solutions that improve usability while also supporting organizational efficiency.

---

## Systems Thinking

One of Marissa’s core strengths is systems thinking.

Many of the products she designs involve complex ecosystems with multiple stakeholders, workflows, and technical dependencies. She approaches these challenges by mapping how users interact with systems and how those systems operate behind the scenes.

This perspective allows her to design solutions that address both the user experience and the operational structure that supports it.

Marissa relies heavily on research to guide product decisions.

Her design process often includes methods such as:

• analytics analysis

These insights help ensure that design decisions are grounded in real user needs rather than assumptions.

Marissa has also developed expertise in designing AI-assisted product experiences.

Her work focuses on creating intelligent systems that support users through suggestions, automation, and guidance while maintaining transparency and user control.

This includes designing guardrails, fallback states, and explainable system behaviors that help users understand how AI features operate.
