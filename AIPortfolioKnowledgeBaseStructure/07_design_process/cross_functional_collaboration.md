# Cross-Functional Collaboration

Successful product design requires close collaboration across multiple disciplines including product management, engineering, research, and business stakeholders.

Marissa Chaplinsky works within cross-functional teams to ensure that design decisions align with both user needs and product strategy.

By working closely with different teams throughout the product development process, Marissa helps ensure that design solutions are both user-centered and technically feasible.

---

## Collaboration with Product Managers

Product managers help define product strategy, priorities, and success metrics.

Marissa frequently collaborates with product managers to translate strategic goals into clear product experiences.

This collaboration often involves:

• defining product requirements  
• prioritizing features and capabilities  
• aligning design decisions with business objectives  
• identifying opportunities for product improvement  

Working closely with product managers helps ensure that design solutions support broader product strategy.

---

## Collaboration with Engineering Teams

Design solutions must operate within the technical architecture of a product.

Marissa collaborates closely with engineering teams to ensure that product concepts translate effectively into production systems.

This collaboration often involves:

• understanding technical constraints and system architecture  
• defining system behavior and interaction logic  
• reviewing prototypes and implementation feasibility  
• aligning design specifications with engineering workflows  

This process helps prevent disconnects between design concepts and technical implementation.

---

## Working with Stakeholders

Product design decisions often affect multiple parts of an organization including leadership, operations teams, and program stakeholders.

Marissa frequently facilitates discussions and workshops that help stakeholders understand design proposals and contribute feedback during decision-making processes.

These conversations help ensure that design solutions remain aligned with organizational priorities.

---

## Facilitating Product Discussions

Designers often act as translators between different disciplines within a product team.

Marissa helps bridge communication between teams by visualizing complex ideas through artifacts such as:

• prototypes  
• user flows  
• journey maps  
• service blueprints  
• system diagrams  

These artifacts help teams understand product concepts, evaluate potential solutions, and align around shared decisions.
