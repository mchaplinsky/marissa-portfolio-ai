\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Healthcare Design Experience\
\
Marissa Chaplinsky has extensive experience designing digital platforms within healthcare and regulated service environments. These systems often involve sensitive user data, complex operational workflows, and diverse user groups including healthcare professionals, administrators, patients, and the public.\
\
Her healthcare design work focuses on improving access to services, simplifying complex processes, and ensuring that platforms remain usable, accessible, and trustworthy for users navigating important health-related information.\
\
Healthcare platforms frequently require balancing usability, compliance considerations, and operational complexity. Marissa approaches these projects by combining user research, service design thinking, and system-level design to create solutions that support both end users and internal teams.\
