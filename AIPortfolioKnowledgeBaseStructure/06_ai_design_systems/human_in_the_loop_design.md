\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Human in the Loop Design\
\
Human-in-the-loop design ensures that users remain actively involved in AI-driven workflows.\
\
Rather than replacing human decision-making, these systems assist users by providing suggestions, automation, and guidance.\
\
Marissa frequently designs AI systems that keep users involved in critical decisions.\
\
---\
\
## Maintaining User Control\
\
Human-in-the-loop systems allow users to:\
\
\'95 review AI outputs  \
\'95 modify results  \
\'95 accept or reject suggestions\
\
This ensures that final outcomes remain under human control.\
\
---\
\
## Supporting Creative Workflows\
\
In creative environments, human oversight is particularly important.\
\
AI can assist by generating ideas, but users ultimately decide how those ideas evolve.\
\
This collaborative relationship helps users feel empowered rather than replaced.\
\
---\
\
## Preventing Over-Automation\
\
Fully automated systems can lead to confusion when users do not understand how results were produced.\
\
Human-in-the-loop systems maintain transparency and allow users to intervene when necessary.\
