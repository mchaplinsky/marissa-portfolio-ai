## Senior Product Designer — TreeRing (EdTech)

Most recently, Marissa worked as a Senior Product Designer designing AI-powered tools and large-scale product experiences for TreeRing, a digital yearbook platform used by millions of students, teachers, and schools.

Her work focused on improving the yearbook creation platform while introducing intelligent automation that helped users design pages faster and with greater confidence.

Key work included leading the design of an AI-powered layout generation system that automatically created page layouts based on photo metadata, layout rules, and print constraints. The system was designed to support non-technical users such as teachers and volunteers while still preserving creative control.

Key contributions included:

- Designing the AI layout generation system  
- Defining AI guardrails and behavioral constraints  
- Creating interaction models for human–AI collaboration  
- Designing UI and visual systems for the editor experience  
- Collaborating with product and engineering on system behavior  

Impact included reducing manual page creation time by roughly **40–60 percent** while improving adoption and reducing usability friction within the editor experience.
