## Visual Hierarchy and Interface Clarity

A core aspect of Marissa’s visual design approach is establishing clear hierarchy so users can immediately understand what actions are available and what information matters most.

She achieves this through careful use of:

- typography systems  
- spacing and layout structure  
- contrast and visual emphasis  
- grouping related information  
- progressive disclosure of complexity  

Strong visual hierarchy reduces cognitive load and allows users to quickly orient themselves within an interface.
