\f0\fs24 \cf0 # CDHBC Regulatory Portal\
\
## Project Overview\
The College of Dental Hygienists of British Columbia (CDHBC) project focused on designing a modern digital platform to replace outdated paper-based administrative processes. The new system introduced administration and registrant portals that allowed staff and licensed professionals to manage licensing, documentation, and program interactions through a centralized system.\
\
## Industry\
Healthcare Regulation\
\
## Organization\
College of Dental Hygienists of British Columbia\
\
## My Role\
Senior UX Designer\
\
## Project Scope\
Design high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes for administration portals, registrant portals, and internal management workflows.\
\
## Problem\
The existing system relied heavily on paper-based processes and fragmented digital tools, making it difficult for administrators and registrants to manage information efficiently.\
\
## Users\
Regulatory administrators  \
Dental hygienists (registrants)  \
Organizational staff\
\
## Research Methods\
Stakeholder interviews  \
Usability testing sessions  \
Workflow analysis\
\
## Design Strategy\
Create scalable administrative dashboards and workflows that could support licensing management while maintaining strong usability for staff handling sensitive regulatory data.\
\
## Key UX Decisions\
Centralized administrative dashboards  \
Advanced search and filtering for registrant records  \
Structured workflow interfaces\
\
## Systems Thinking\
The portal required handling large datasets and sensitive licensing information while supporting operational workflows used by regulatory staff.\
\

Accessible navigation structures\
